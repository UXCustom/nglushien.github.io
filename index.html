A Little About Me

I've been working in the web design/development industry for more than 11 years. My career began as a layout designer and flash animator. From there I became a front-end developer where I also worked on videography and post production. Currently I work on CMS template development. This gives me the experience of working for a large corporation with complex team structures and development environments. Over the last few years I've shifted my focus to UX design. The field involves my love of psychology with my passion for design. This is an exciting time to be in this industry and I'm glad to be a part of it.
